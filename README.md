
# SafeNet AI

Think Before You Click!



## 🔍 Overview
As digital payments and online services grow, cyber fraud has become increasingly common, and many users struggle to spot scams before it is too late.

SafeNet AI is an intelligent digital safety assistant that helps users detect and understand potential scams before taking action. By analyzing messages, emails, websites, QR codes, and job offers, the system provides risk assessments, explanations, and safety recommendations to promote safer online behavior.
## ⚙️ How It Works

### Content Analysis

Users can submit:

* Screenshots
* Emails
* Website URLs
* QR Codes
* Job Advertisements
* Payment Requests

### AI-Powered Detection

The system analyzes content for:

* Urgent or threatening language
* Requests for OTPs, passwords, or sensitive information
* Suspicious URLs and domains
* Fake branding or impersonation attempts
* Unrealistic promises and rewards
* Requests for advance payments

### Risk Assessment

Generates:

* Risk Score
* Threat Classification (Safe, Suspicious, High Risk)
* Explainable reasoning behind the decision

### Recommendation Engine

Provides actionable guidance such as:

* Verify
* Ignore
* Block
* Report
* Contact official support channels
